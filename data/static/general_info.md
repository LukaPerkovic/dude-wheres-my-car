# Neo-Terra Skyport Parking: General Information

Nestled beneath the neon-lit cantilevers of the Helix District, the Neo-Terra Skyport Parking Complex is the primary multi-level parking facility serving the Skyport arrival concourses and the surrounding mixed-use city blocks. Designed to accommodate the diverse transportation ecosystem of the Cyberpunk metropolis, the facility spans fifteen automated tiers with an overall capacity of 4,800 vehicle bays. Of these, 2,600 bays are dedicated to standard groundcars (Class G1 through G4), 900 bays are retrofitted for hovercraft coupes and lev-bikes (Class H2), 400 bays serve heavy freight haulers up to 14 metric tons, 300 bays are secured pods for autonomous corporate shuttles, and an additional 600 bays are modular flex spaces that can be reassigned based on daily demand analytics.

Access control is orchestrated by the Synapse Gate System, a biometric and RFID hybrid that recognizes registered drives, corporate fleet tags, and permit microchips sewn into license fabrics. Walk-in patrons can also obtain temporary QR wristbands via the on-site kiosk network. Vehicles exceeding 3.5 meters in height must request pre-clearance through the facility’s mesh portal to ensure compatibility with ceiling-mounted mag-lock guides. Subterranean levels L1 through L4 house combustion-tolerant bays with enhanced ventilation for classic petrol collectors, while upper decks U10 through U15 are dedicated to anti-grav landing pads with magnetic tethering pylons.

Parking rules are enforced by the Civic Grid Authority in tandem with the facility’s own Sentinel Drones. Standard stay is billed in 30-minute increments with a maximum continuous occupancy of 72 hours unless an extended-stay permit is logged. Unauthorized modifications, weaponized drone deployment, and illicit bio-modded fauna are strictly prohibited on the premises. Overnight storage of unregistered cybernetic cargo triggers an automatic audit. Noise emissions above 85 decibels between 23:00 and 06:00 Neo-Terra Standard Time result in immediate citation. Charging stations, both electro and plasma inductive, are located on every even-numbered level; use is limited to 120 minutes per session during peak hours.

Pricing varies per tiers and per on-peak/off-peak durations throught the day. Some of the tiers are: Standard Groundcar, Hovercraft and Lev-Bikes, Heavy Freight, Autonomous Shuttle Pods, and Flex Bays.

Payment methods are seamlessly integrated into the city’s credit lattice. Patrons may authorize charges via CredChips, Omni-ID implants, encrypted mobile wallets (MembranePay, LumaCredits), or corporate ledger contracts. The parking hub also supports interplanetary credit exchange for off-world visitors, applying real-time conversion rates. A “Street-Level Access” counter in the main atrium accepts physical currency (polymer notes and micro-minted coins) for legacy users, though a 5% processing fee applies.

Security features reflect Neo-Terra’s high standards: 24/7 aerial patrols by Vanta-class drones equipped with thermal and spectral analytics, biometric entry scans, and a distributed ledger that records every ingress, egress, and payment handshake. The facility is wrapped in a Faraday mesh to prevent signal skimming and rides on a redundant power grid with anti-surge capacitors to withstand electromagnetic sabotage. In emergency scenarios, the Skyport Parking Complex can partition sections into sealed zones, deploy vented sleeping pods, and summon MedTech units through the city’s rapid response net. Patrons benefit from the Resident Guardian AI, “Aegis-19,” which provides real-time alerts, escorts, and route visualizations through any standard Neural HUD, ensuring the parking experience remains safe, efficient, and tuned to the rhythm of Neo-Terra’s ever-shining skyline.
