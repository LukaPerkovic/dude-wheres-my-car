# Neo-Terra Skyport Parking: Booking Process

## Reservation Channels
Parking reservations can be made through three primary interfaces:
1. **Aegis Portal (Web/App):** Access via the Neo-Terra Civic Grid or download the “Aegis-19 Parking” app. Log in with your Omni-ID implant, CredChip, or social trust profile. Corporate fleet managers can integrate API access for bulk scheduling.
2. **Synapse Kiosks (On-Site):** Holographic kiosks in the Helix Promenade and Skyport Arrivals Plaza support instant bookings. Scan your biometric signature or upload a temporary profile via QR wristband.
3. **Mesh Concierge (Voice/AI):** Dial Gridline 8-19 from any comms device and interact with the Resident Guardian AI. Voice-print validation is required; the AI can generate booking confirmations, modify stays, or trigger special accommodations.

## Booking Steps
1. **Select Stay Parameters:** Choose vehicle class (G1-G4 groundcar, H2 hovercraft, freight, autonomous pod, flex bay), arrival window, and estimated departure. Standard reservations allow for arrival within a two-hour window; beyond that, the slot may be reassigned.
2. **Assign Enhancements:** Optional add-ons include reserved charging stations, valet drone retrieval, security escort, climate-controlled bays, or maintenance diagnostics.
3. **Confirm Payment Method:** Link a payment source (CredChip, MembranePay wallet, corporate ledger, physical currency deposit). Deposits equal to one hour’s rate are pre-authorized.
4. **Receive Access Credentials:** A digital Boarding Pass (DBP) is issued, embedding a unique quantum checksum. For manual entry, the system prints a heat-sensitive code strip. All credentials sync to the Synapse Gate System.

## Check-In Procedure
Upon arrival, approach the designated ingress lane. Gate sensors scan the DBP; alternatively, present your Omni-ID or wristband. Vehicle class verification occurs via lidar and spectral imaging. Once cleared, overhead holopanels guide you to the assigned bay. Valet drone service, if selected, will rendezvous at Buffer Zone Delta for key handoff or remote pilot authentication.

## Check-Out & Extensions
Departing patrons drive to egress lanes where the system calculates final charges. Any add-on usage, overtime, or service fees are itemized on the holo-receipt. Extensions can be initiated up to 30 minutes before scheduled exit via the app or by signaling the nearest holo-pylon. The Aegis AI evaluates bay availability and may reassign you to another level if capacity is constrained.

## Cancellation & Modification Policy
- **Flexible Rate Bookings:** Cancel or modify up to one hour before arrival without penalty. After that, 50% of the first hour’s fee is retained.
- **Premium Priority Slots:** Require four-hour notice to avoid full forfeiture of the deposit. Priority users gain guaranteed placement and accelerated check-in lanes.
- **Corporate Contracts:** Governed by service-level agreements; typically, a six-hour cancellation window applies.
No-shows trigger a two-hour minimum charge to cover bay opportunity costs. Refunds are disbursed within 12 grid-hours via the original payment method.

## Pricing Tiers (Baseline Rates per Hour)
- **Standard Groundcar (G1-G3):** 24 credits off-peak / 36 peak.
- **Hovercraft & Lev-Bikes (H2):** 32 credits off-peak / 45 peak.
- **Heavy Freight (up to 14 metric tons):** 58 credits flat; includes loading dock access.
- **Autonomous Shuttle Pods:** 40 credits; eligible for fleet discounts.
- **Flex Bays:** Dynamic pricing ranging 28–50 credits depending on configuration.
Add-ons: charging (6 credits/session), valet drone (12 credits), security escort (9 credits), climate stabilization (5 credits).

## Special Accommodations
The facility supports adaptive vehicles for augmented or mobility-limited users, including widened bay grids, low-profile ramps, and tactile floor guidance. Biohazard containment bays can be pre-booked for cryo-pods or sensitive cargo, subject to inspection fees. VIP Skyport passengers may request the “Aurora Corridor,” a direct mag-walk link to private boarding gates, complete with concierge android assistance. Military, civic responders, and certified med-transport operators receive prioritized access during emergencies, coordinated through the city’s Rapid Response lattice.

All reservations are enforced by the Aegis-19 Guardian, ensuring transparent audit trails, seamless customer experience, and adherence to Neo-Terra’s high-security protocols.
